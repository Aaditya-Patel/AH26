# Seller Registration Process - Indian Carbon Market

## Overview
To register as a carbon credit seller in India, entities must complete project registration with BEE, select an approved methodology, choose a verification agency, and obtain BEE approval.

## Step 1: Project Registration

### Required Documents
- **Project Proposal** - Detailed project proposal describing the carbon reduction project
- **Methodology Document** - Document outlining the methodology to be used for calculating emissions reduction

### How to Obtain
Submit your project proposal to BEE through the Indian Carbon Market portal. The proposal should include project description, expected emission reductions, and methodology details. BEE reviews and approves projects based on compliance with approved methodologies.

## Step 2: Methodology Selection

### Required Documents
- **Methodology Selection Form** - Form indicating the chosen methodology from approved list

### How to Obtain
BEE has approved eight offset methodologies for carbon credit projects. Review the approved methodologies and select the one applicable to your project. Submit the methodology selection form through the BEE portal. The approved methodologies cover various sectors including energy efficiency, renewable energy, and waste management.

## Step 3: Verification Agency

### Required Documents
- **Agency Agreement** - Agreement with an Accredited Carbon Verification (ACV) Agency

### How to Obtain
Select an Accredited Carbon Verification (ACV) Agency from the list of BEE-accredited agencies. Contact the agency and enter into an agreement for validation and verification services. ACV agencies are authorized by BEE to validate and verify carbon reduction projects.

### Accredited Agencies
BEE maintains a list of accredited verification agencies. You can find the list on the BEE website or contact BEE directly at sdiddi@beeindia.gov.in and meenakshi@beeindia.gov.in.

## Step 4: Document Submission

### Required Documents
- **MRV Plan** - Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification plan
- **Baseline Study** - Baseline emissions study for the project
- **Financial Documents** - Project financial statements and funding documentation

### How to Obtain
- **MRV Plan**: Develop a comprehensive MRV plan that outlines how emissions will be monitored, reported, and verified. This should align with the selected methodology and BEE requirements.

- **Baseline Study**: Conduct a baseline study to establish the emissions levels before project implementation. This serves as the reference point for calculating emission reductions.

- **Financial Documents**: Prepare project financial statements, funding documentation, and cost-benefit analysis. These documents demonstrate project viability.

## Step 5: BEE Approval

After submitting all required documents and completing validation by the ACV agency, BEE reviews and approves the project. Approval typically takes 30-60 days after complete submission.

## Business Registration Requirements

### PAN Card
- Apply through Income Tax Department (www.incometax.gov.in)
- Required documents: Proof of identity, address, and date of birth
- Processing time: 15-20 days

### GST Registration
- Register through GST portal (www.gst.gov.in)
- Required documents:
  - PAN card
  - Proof of business constitution (Partnership Deed, Certificate of Incorporation, etc.)
  - Proof of principal place of business (electricity bill, rent agreement)
  - Bank account details (cancelled cheque)
  - Photographs of directors/partners
- Processing time: 3-7 working days

### Company Registration
- Register with Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)
- Required documents:
  - Memorandum of Association (MOA)
  - Articles of Association (AOA)
  - PAN and Aadhaar of directors
  - Proof of registered office address
- Processing time: 7-15 days
