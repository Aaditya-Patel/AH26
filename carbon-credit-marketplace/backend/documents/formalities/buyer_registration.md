# Buyer Registration Process - Indian Carbon Market

## Overview
To register as a buyer in the Indian Carbon Market (ICM), entities must complete registration with the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) and the Grid Controller of India (GCI) registry.

## Step 1: Company Registration

### Required Documents
- **Company Registration Certificate** - Certificate of Incorporation issued by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)
- **PAN Card** - Permanent Account Number (PAN) of the business entity

### How to Obtain
- **Company Registration Certificate**: Register your company with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) through the MCA portal. Submit required documents including Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA). The Certificate of Incorporation is issued upon successful registration.

- **PAN Card**: Apply for PAN through the Income Tax Department website (www.incometax.gov.in) or through authorized agents. You will need proof of identity, proof of address, and proof of date of birth. The PAN card is issued within 15-20 days.

## Step 2: GCI Registry Account

### Required Documents
- **Company Details** - Complete company information including name, address, registration number
- **Contact Information** - Authorized representative details, email, phone number

### How to Obtain
Visit the GCI registry portal at carbonregistry.nccf.in and complete the registration form. You will need to provide your company registration details and contact information. The registry account is created after document verification.

## Step 3: Document Verification

### Required Documents
- **GST Certificate** - Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) certificate
- **Address Proof** - Utility bill, property tax receipt, or ownership deed for business address

### How to Obtain
- **GST Certificate**: Register for GST through the GST portal (www.gst.gov.in) using your PAN. Required documents include proof of business constitution, proof of principal place of business, bank account details, and photographs of authorized signatories. GSTIN certificate is issued within 3-7 working days.

- **Address Proof**: Use electricity bill, property tax receipt, or ownership deed for owned property. For rented property, provide rent/lease agreement along with electricity bill or property tax receipt.

## Step 4: Bank Details

### Required Documents
- **Cancelled Cheque** - Bank cheque with account details
- **Bank Statement** - Recent bank statement showing account number and business name

### How to Obtain
- **Cancelled Cheque**: Get a cheque leaf from your business bank account, write "CANCELLED" across it, and submit. This provides account number and bank details.

- **Bank Statement**: Download from your bank's net banking portal or request from bank branch. Ensure it shows the business name and account number.

## Step 5: Final Approval

After submitting all required documents, wait for BEE and GCI approval. This typically takes 7-14 business days for document verification and account activation.
