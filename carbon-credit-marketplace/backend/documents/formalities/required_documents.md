# Required Documents for Carbon Credit Registration - India

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive information about all documents required for carbon credit registration, verification, and compliance in India.

## Business Registration Documents

### PAN Card (Permanent Account Number)

**Purpose**: Tax identification number required for all business entities

**How to Obtain**:
1. Visit Income Tax Department website: www.incometax.gov.in
2. Fill out Form 49A (for Indian entities) or Form 49AA (for foreign entities)
3. Submit required documents:
   - Proof of identity (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, etc.)
   - Proof of address (Utility bill, Bank statement, etc.)
   - Proof of date of birth (Birth certificate, School certificate, etc.)
   - Recent photograph
4. Pay applicable fees
5. Processing time: 15-20 days

**Required for**: Both buyers and sellers

### GST Registration Certificate

**Purpose**: Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) required for businesses with turnover above threshold

**How to Obtain**:
1. Visit GST portal: www.gst.gov.in
2. Complete Part-A of GST registration form
3. Verify email and mobile number with OTP
4. Complete Part-B with required details
5. Upload required documents:
   - PAN card
   - Proof of business constitution (Partnership Deed, Certificate of Incorporation, etc.)
   - Proof of principal place of business (Electricity bill, Rent agreement, etc.)
   - Bank account details (Cancelled cheque, Bank statement)
   - Photographs of directors/partners
   - Authorization letter/Board resolution
   - Digital Signature Certificate (for companies/LLPs)
6. Submit application
7. Processing time: 3-7 working days

**Required for**: Both buyers and sellers

### Company Registration Certificate

**Purpose**: Legal recognition of business entity

**Types**:
- Sole Proprietorship: No separate registration required
- Partnership: Partnership Deed required
- Limited Liability Partnership (LLP): Certificate of Incorporation from MCA
- Private/Public Limited Company: Certificate of Incorporation from MCA

**How to Obtain (For Companies/LLPs)**:
1. Visit Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) portal: www.mca.gov.in
2. Reserve company name through RUN service
3. File incorporation forms:
   - SPICe+ form (for companies)
   - Form FiLLiP (for LLPs)
4. Upload required documents:
   - Memorandum of Association (MOA)
   - Articles of Association (AOA)
   - Director/Partner details and documents
   - Proof of registered office address
   - PAN and Aadhaar of directors/partners
5. Pay stamp duty and registration fees
6. Processing time: 7-15 days

**Required for**: Both buyers and sellers

## Address Proof Documents

**Accepted Documents**:
- Electricity bill (not older than 2 months)
- Property tax receipt
- Ownership deed (for owned property)
- Rent/Lease agreement with electricity bill or property tax receipt (for rented property)
- No Objection Certificate (NOC) from property owner (if property is used by third party)

**How to Obtain**: Contact your electricity provider, municipal corporation, or property owner

**Required for**: GST registration, company registration

## Bank Account Documents

**Types**:
- Cancelled cheque
- Bank statement (not older than 3 months)
- First page of bank passbook

**How to Obtain**:
- **Cancelled Cheque**: Write "CANCELLED" across a cheque leaf from your business account
- **Bank Statement**: Download from net banking or request from bank branch
- **Passbook**: Get updated from bank branch

**Required for**: GST registration, BEE registration, GCI account setup

## Seller-Specific Documents

### Project Proposal

**Purpose**: Detailed description of carbon reduction project

**Contents**:
- Project description and location
- Technology/process to be used
- Expected emission reductions
- Timeline and implementation plan
- Financial projections
- Environmental benefits

**How to Prepare**: Use BEE guidelines and template available on BEE website

**Required for**: Seller registration - Step 1

### Methodology Document

**Purpose**: Document outlining approved methodology for calculating emissions reduction

**How to Obtain**: 
- Review BEE's approved methodologies (8 methodologies available)
- Select applicable methodology for your project
- Prepare methodology document following BEE format
- Available on BEE website: www.beeindia.gov.in

**Required for**: Seller registration - Step 2

### ACV Agency Agreement

**Purpose**: Agreement with Accredited Carbon Verification Agency

**How to Obtain**:
1. Review list of BEE-accredited ACV agencies
2. Contact selected agency
3. Request quotation for validation and verification services
4. Sign service agreement
5. Agency details available from BEE: sdiddi@beeindia.gov.in

**Required for**: Seller registration - Step 3

### MRV Plan

**Purpose**: Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification plan

**Contents**:
- Monitoring methodologies
- Data collection procedures
- Quality assurance measures
- Documentation requirements
- Personnel responsibilities

**How to Prepare**: Align with selected methodology and BEE guidelines

**Required for**: Seller registration - Step 4, MRV compliance - Step 1

### Baseline Study

**Purpose**: Establish emissions baseline before project implementation

**How to Obtain**: Conduct baseline emissions assessment following approved methodology guidelines

**Required for**: Seller registration - Step 4

## MRV Compliance Documents

### Data Collection Forms

**Purpose**: Standardized forms for recording emissions data

**How to Obtain**: 
- Use BEE-prescribed forms from BEE portal
- Or develop custom forms aligned with monitoring plan

**Required for**: MRV compliance - Step 2

### Emissions Report

**Purpose**: Comprehensive GHG emissions report

**Contents**:
- Executive summary
- Methodology applied
- Baseline and project emissions
- Emission reductions achieved
- Data quality assessment
- Supporting documentation

**How to Prepare**: Follow BEE reporting format and guidelines

**Required for**: MRV compliance - Step 3

### Verification Report (Form B)

**Purpose**: Third-party verification certificate from ACV agency

**How to Obtain**: 
- Engage accredited ACV agency
- Agency conducts verification
- Agency issues Form B (Certificate of Verification)
- Submitted by agency to BEE and SDA

**Required for**: MRV compliance - Step 4

### Performance Assessment Document (Form A)

**Purpose**: Compliance status declaration by obligated entity

**Contents**:
- Compliance status
- Verified emissions summary
- Actions taken for compliance
- Supporting documentation

**How to Obtain**: Prepare and submit to BEE after receiving Form B

**Required for**: MRV compliance - Step 5

## Document Preparation Tips

1. **Consistency**: Ensure information is consistent across all documents (company name, address, registration numbers)

2. **Self-Attestation**: Most documents require self-attestation

3. **Validity**: Check validity periods - some documents expire (bank statements, electricity bills)

4. **Digital Signatures**: Companies and LLPs need Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) for online submissions

5. **Translation**: If documents are in regional languages, provide English translations

6. **Notarization**: Some documents may require notarization - check specific requirements
